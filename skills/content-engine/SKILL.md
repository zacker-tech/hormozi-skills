# Content Engine

**Command:** `/content-engine`

**From:** $100M Leads

**When to use:** Building an audience, growing on social media, or creating a content-driven lead generation system.

## What it does

Designs a content engine using Hormozi's framework — a repeatable system that turns your expertise into leads on autopilot.

## The framework

**The Content Engine has 3 parts:**

1. **Core content** — One long-form piece per week (YouTube, podcast, newsletter)
   - This is your "pillar" — everything else comes from this

2. **Derivative content** — 3-5 short-form pieces per core piece
   - Clips, tweets, carousels, quotes extracted from the core

3. **Distribution** — Push to every channel your audience lives on

**Hormozi's content rule:**
> Document > Create. Share what you're doing, not what you think sounds good.

**Content types that build trust fastest:**
1. Results you achieved (with proof)
2. Mistakes you made (with lessons)
3. Process you follow (step by step)
4. Opinions that polarize (take a real stance)

## Prompt template

```
My niche: [what I build/do]
My audience: [who follows me]
Platforms I'm on: [X, YouTube, Instagram, etc.]
Time I can dedicate: [X hours/week]
My biggest recent win or lesson: [describe it]

Build my content engine:
1. Recommend my core content format
2. Give me 5 content ideas based on my recent win/lesson
3. Show how to break idea #1 into derivative content for each platform
4. Write the hook for the best piece
```
