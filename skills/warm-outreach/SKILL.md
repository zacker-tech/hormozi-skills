# Warm Outreach

**Command:** `/warm-outreach`

**From:** $100M Leads

**When to use:** Getting your first customers, relaunching a product, or closing deals without paid ads.

## What it does

Builds a warm outreach sequence using Hormozi's method — reaching people who already know you before going cold.

## The framework

Warm outreach order (highest to lowest conversion):
1. **Past customers** — already bought from you, easiest to sell again
2. **Warm leads** — expressed interest but didn't buy
3. **Personal network** — friends, colleagues, former coworkers
4. **Social followers** — engaged with your content
5. **Cold outreach** — only after exhausting the above

**The 3-step warm message:**
1. **Compliment** — something specific and genuine (not flattery)
2. **Bridge** — why you're reaching out, connect to them specifically
3. **Ask** — one clear, low-friction ask (call, feedback, referral)

**Never:**
- Lead with the pitch
- Send a wall of text
- Ask for too much in the first message

## Prompt template

```
My product: [describe it]
Price: $[X]
Who I'm reaching out to: [relationship — ex-colleague, Twitter follower, past customer]
Their situation: [what you know about them]

Write a warm outreach message:
1. Using the Compliment → Bridge → Ask framework
2. Under 5 sentences
3. With a clear, low-friction CTA
4. Tone: [casual / professional]
```
