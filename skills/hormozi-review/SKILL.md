# Hormozi Review

**Command:** `/hormozi-review`

**From:** Hormozi trilogy ($100M Offers, $100M Leads, $100M Money Models)

**When to use:** Gut-checking any business decision — pricing, product, marketing, hiring, or strategy.

## What it does

Applies the core principles from all three Hormozi books to any business decision. Like having Hormozi review your move before you make it.

## The checklist

Before any major decision, run it through:

**From $100M Offers:**
- [ ] Is this offer specific enough? (vague offers don't sell)
- [ ] Does it solve a painful, urgent problem?
- [ ] Is the dream outcome crystal clear?
- [ ] Have I reversed the risk for the buyer?
- [ ] Would I feel stupid saying no to this?

**From $100M Leads:**
- [ ] Do I know exactly where my customer is?
- [ ] Am I using warm channels before cold?
- [ ] Am I mastering one acquisition channel before adding another?
- [ ] Does my content document results, not just share opinions?
- [ ] Is my lead magnet delivering value in under 10 minutes?

**From $100M Money Models:**
- [ ] Am I charging enough? (if in doubt, raise the price)
- [ ] Is my retention strategy stronger than my acquisition strategy?
- [ ] Do I know my LTV vs CAC ratio?
- [ ] Am I optimizing for profit margin, not just revenue?
- [ ] Is this decision making my model simpler or more complex?

## Prompt template

```
Decision I'm considering: [describe it]
Context: [current MRR, stage, what problem this solves]
My concern: [what I'm not sure about]

Run this through the full Hormozi Review:
1. Check it against all 3 books' frameworks
2. Flag any red flags
3. Give me a clear yes/no recommendation
4. If yes: what's the first action?
5. If no: what should I do instead?
```
